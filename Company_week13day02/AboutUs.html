About Us

We are here to build a safe intercity bus network in the country.
Safety is not expensive, it is priceless. Therefore, safety of our customers and crew members is of utmost importance to us. By design, our ecosystem redefines safety in public transport space.Some of our key features are:
Safe Buses – Panic Button; CCTV Cameras; GPS enabled premium buses; Advanced Braking System & Vehicle Rollover protection up to 42 degrees.
Safe Routes – Geofencing; Ride tracking and sharing; Pre-defined pit stops.
Safe Travel – Verified and trained staff; Central command center; Data privacy provided to all app users.
